Build a Chinese voice assistant in megafish (Qwen3-Omni, Megatron) that converses by ear: it listens to user speech and replies with low-latency, listenable audio and natural turn-taking. Half-duplex first (fast, crutch-free turn switching) as the concrete path to full listen-while-speak duplex. Bar for "done" on this milestone: free-running voice-in -> voice-out replies with low CER and sub-second turn-taking with no eval crutch, built on the frozen 25Hz / 10-codebook codec and the causal streaming encoder, initialized from the production S2 voice checkpoint. Pillars: codec-contract, turn-layout, streaming-encoder, endpoint-turn-taking.
